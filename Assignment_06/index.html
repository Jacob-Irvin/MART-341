Homemade Oreos

Ingredients

    Oreos

  • 1 and 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 cups cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 lareg egg
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

    • Creme Filling

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup vegtable shortening
  • 1 and 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Mix flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in bowl
  2. Slowly mix butter, sugar, brown sugar, egg, and vanilla extract in a bowl on medium speed until dough is formed. Cover dough with plastic wrap and chill for 30 minutes
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  4. Roll dough into small balls and flatten, bake for 6-8 minutes
  5. Allow cool for 5 minutes while making creme filling
  6. In small bowl mix butter, shortening, vanilla extract, and sugar until you've reached a thick, creamy consistency.
  7. Lastly, spread creme filling in betwen 2 cookies and enjoy
