About the Author

I chose this "project" because fashion is something that I am infatuated with. I am always observing how others interpret fashion and running through new ways to put pieces together in my head. What started out as sort of a hobby collecting shoes blossomed into a lifestyle and a way for me to express myself more so than I can with anything else. I have a love for fashion so I took this project as an opportunity to share the process that goes through my head when I'm doing something that I love.
